The Smart Vault execution layer for OpenClaw

Why This Exists

OpenClaw is built around a simple idea: an autonomous monitor that actually does things. It sends emails, books meetings, and runs system commands. But the moment your monitor touches financial liquidity, ‚Äúactually doing things‚Äù becomes a liability.

AlphaClaw solves this by sitting between your OpenClaw instance and the Solana blockchain. It enforces hard limits on trade sizes, daily exposure, and slippage cap. It scores migrations for liquidity depth before your engine can act on them.

How It Connects

AlphaClaw operates as a specialized skill for your local OpenClaw gateway (ws://127.0.0.1:18789). Once standard handshake is verified, AlphaClaw pulls your node's active skills and walks you through security rule configuration. Once deployed, the monitor runs continuously‚Äîscanning DEXes and enforcing your limits on every automated action.

Execution Strategy Modes

üõ°Ô∏è Passive Sentry

Manual trades only. Engine never buys on its own. Best for situational monitoring.

‚öñÔ∏è Market Seeker

Moderate mode. Executes on confirmed liquid moves and arbitrage gaps > 4%.

üéØ Apex Hunter

Aggressive mode. Actively hunts yield, micro-arbitrage, and market dips.

üî• Sniper Elite

High intensity. Snipes new liquid migrations and early-stage memecoin launches.

Safety Guardrails

  • Max Trade Size: Default 0.5 SOL limit per single execution.
  • Daily Exposure: Total SOL limit per 24-hour rolling window (Default 2.0 SOL).
  • Slippage Cap: Maximum acceptable execution price variance (Default 1%).

Non-Custodial Design

AlphaClaw is a rule-based execution layer. Your Solana wallet connection is used for identity and signature verification only‚Äînot for custodial private key access. Your keys never leave your local machine.
